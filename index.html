About Me

Environmental Engineer | AI & IoT Systems Developer | University of Michigan

I'm a high school senior at Huron High School with a passion for environmental engineering and wastewater treatment innovation. My work focuses on using artificial intelligence and IoT systems to revolutionize how we approach water treatment, making existing systems more efficient, sustainable, and intelligent.

Through dual enrollment at the University of Michigan, I pursue graduate-level coursework in environmental data analysis while conducting hands-on research. My signature project involves developing an intelligent aeration system for microbial fuel cells in wastewater treatment, using machine learning optimization to maximize energy efficiency and treatment effectiveness.

Beyond research, I'm an accomplished double bassist who has competed internationally and performed across the US and Europe. I also founded SteamFun4Kids, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to making STEM education accessible to underserved communities.
