About

High school senior at Huron High School, passionate about environmental engineering and wastewater treatment innovation. Through dual enrollment at the University of Michigan, I pursue graduate-level coursework while conducting research.

Beyond research, I'm an accomplished double bassist competing internationally and founder of SteamFun4Kids, a 501(c)(3) nonprofit making STEM education accessible to underserved communities.
